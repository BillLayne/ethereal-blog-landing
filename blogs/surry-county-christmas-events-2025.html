üåü Insider Tips for Surry County Christmas Events

After 20+ years of attending these events, here are my best tips to make your holiday experience stress-free and magical:

Parking & Arrival Tips

  • Mayberry Parade: Arrive 90+ minutes early for good spots. Park behind the Andy Griffith Museum or on side streets off Main.
  • Elkin Parade: Arrive 45 minutes early. Park along Market Street or in the municipal lot.
  • Tree Lightings: These fill up fast! Arrive 30-45 minutes before the scheduled lighting time.

What to Bring

  • Lawn chairs and blankets for comfortable parade viewing
  • Hot beverages in travel mugs (evenings get cold!)
  • Cash for vendors and donations
  • Flashlights for walking back to your car after dark events
  • Camera for capturing those magical moments

December in Surry County averages highs in the mid-40s and lows in the upper 20s. Layer up! Evening events can feel significantly colder, especially near the mountains.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest Christmas event in Surry County?

The Mayberry Christmas Parade in Mount Airy (December 6) is the largest and most-attended event, drawing visitors from across the Southeast. It typically attracts 15,000-20,000 spectators and features dozens of floats, marching bands, and Santa himself!

Are these events free?

Yes! Nearly every event on this list is completely free. The only exceptions are ticketed theater performances like "A Mayberry Christmas" at the Andy Griffith Playhouse ($10-15). Parades, tree lightings, and community celebrations are all free to attend.

What if it rains or snows on parade day?

Most parades have rain dates scheduled one week later. Light rain typically doesn't cancel events, but heavy rain or ice might. Follow local Facebook pages and news outlets for last-minute updates. The Mayberry parade's rain date is December 13.

Where can my kids meet Santa?

Santa appears at nearly every tree lighting ceremony and at the end of every parade. The best photo opportunities are typically at the Mount Airy tree lighting (December 5) and various "Breakfast with Santa" events hosted by churches and civic organizations. Check local Facebook groups for pop-up Santa visits at stores and restaurants throughout December.

What's the weather typically like in December?

Surry County averages highs in the mid-40s¬∞F and lows in the upper 20s¬∞F during December. We typically see 1-2 snow events per month, though they're often light dustings. The mountains can be 5-10 degrees colder, especially in the evenings. Layer up and bring warm beverages!

Are there any Christmas events on Christmas Day itself?

Christmas Day (December 25) is typically a quiet family day in Surry County. Most public events occur in the weeks leading up to Christmas. Churches hold Christmas Eve candlelight services on December 24, and some host Christmas morning services as well. Restaurants and businesses are generally closed on Christmas Day.
